CV Craft Pro Help

Getting Started

Follow these steps to create your professional tech CV:

  1. Fill in your personal information
  2. Write a compelling professional summary (or use our AI to help)
  3. Add your work experience with key achievements
  4. List your technical skills and educational background
  5. Preview your CV and make adjustments
  6. Export or download your finished CV

Tech Resume Best Practices

  • Tailor your resume to the specific job you're applying for
  • Quantify your achievements with metrics and results
  • Focus on relevant technical skills and experience
  • Use industry-standard terminology
  • Keep your resume concise (1-2 pages)
  • Include relevant projects or portfolio links
  • Proofread carefully for errors
